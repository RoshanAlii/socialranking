Inputs and accuracy
The follower count comes from the public Instagram profile surface. Cadence comes from a separate posts query filtered to 31 days. The calculation counts unique posts authored by the profile inside the exact 30-day snapshot window. A recent pinned post counts; an old pinned post does not. Duplicate rows and posts owned by another account are removed. When a query fails or reaches its result limit before the cutoff, the dashboard shows “—” instead of estimating.
