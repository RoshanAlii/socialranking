Measurement rules
Instagram only. The ingestion fetches up to 100 recent posts per profile. Engagement, cadence, participation, top-post and momentum rankings include a profile only when its fetched feed reaches the start of the common 30-day window. Engagement additionally needs at least three posts. Pinned posts are excluded. Weekly growth appears only when a baseline between five and nine days old exists.
