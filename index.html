How this snapshot stays fair and honest
Public snapshot, not Insights

Numbers come from public profile and post surfaces through the named provider. They are point-in-time observations, not official Instagram Insights or real-time telemetry.

One comparable window

Cadence and engagement require the same trailing 30 days. A profile is withheld when the post query cannot prove complete coverage of that window.

Typical engagement

The median supported interactions per eligible post is divided by followers. At least three comparable posts are required, so a single viral post cannot define typical performance.

Current pinned-post rule

The version-3 cadence engine counts a pinned post only when its actual publication date is inside the window; old pins fall outside by date. Missing values remain unknown, never zero.
